Lucid Dreaming
To lucid dream it means you are consciously
aware that you are dreaming
and are able to control the dream.
People believe that music
can trigger lucid dreams.
How to:
Lucid Dream
.
1) Keep a record of your dreams.
An easy way to remember would be to
write them in a notebook by your bed.
2) Use reality checks frequently
(If you do it enough you'll follow the habit in your dreams.)
-Pinch your nose, close mouth, check to see if you can breath.
-Are your hands and feet distorted or clear?
3) Repeat "I will be aware that I'm dreaming."
(Each time you fall asleep.)
This is a process called:
Mnemonic Induction to Lucid Dreaming (or MILD)
Mnemonic Induction means "using memoy aids"
4) Look for recurring things that happen in your dreams.
5) Drift back to sleep when awakened from a dream.
